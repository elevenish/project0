About Me

My name is CiCi Yu, and I'm a public high school math teacher in the Greater Boston area. I use a problem-based, discussion, and inquiry approach to make math suck a little less for math-phobic students. While I am most passionate about teaching geometry, I've also taught Algebra I, Statistics, and AP Calculus AB. At various times during the school year, I serve as a mentor to pre-service math teachers.

I proudly hold a B.A. in Psychology and routinely build time for "lessons of self" in my classes. I believe that secondary education is as much about helping students grapple with culture and identity as much as it is about learning mathematical content. I am a recipient of the Sontag Prize in Urban Education (2017) and a co-creator/designer of Play With Your Math.
