I am involved in a number of side projects.

  • Play With Your Math

    • I believe that math is fun first. With my former colleague, Joey Kelly, we co-curate and design math poster problems that encourage students to play with their math.

  • After The Dance

    • I did technical direction for a project film called After The Dance by Jun Shepard (currently seeking music rights).

  • Lincoln Douglas Debate

    • I coach my high school's Lincoln Douglas debate team.

Additionally, I am interested in mathematical modeling and finding ways to teach modeling outside the traditional textbook problems that encourage "fake-world" math.
